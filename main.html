Why choose us?

Quality

We consistently strive to give you the highest quality work. Our doctors are experienced, award winning Seattle dentists, who continuously train in the latest dentist technologies. We value integrity and build our relationships on trust.

Convenience & Comfort

Our comprehensive dental services will save you time and worry. We offer extended hours, a convenient North Seattle location, free parking and a comforting environment to relax you during your visit. We also understand that dental visits can be a source of apprehension for many people. That’s why we will make your experience as comfortable and pleasant as possible.

Expertise & Technology

We understand that technology and continuing education means more accurate diagnoses, improves your dental treatments and speeds recovery. This is why we invest in the latest technology and continue our education on an on-going basis so that we can stay up-to-date on the newest dental techniques, which includes cosmetic dentistry. We care enough for our patients to make sure that “new and novel” is equal or better than “tried and true”.
